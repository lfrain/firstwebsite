Crum and Forster

Crum and Forster provides specialty and standard commercial lines insurance products through our admitted and surplus lines insurance companies. We distribute products through approximately 1,500 authorized retail and wholesale brokers across the United States.

Products are available in the following areas:

  • Commercial casualty
  • Environmental
  • Energy exploration and production
  • Group personal umbrella
  • Ocean marine
  • Product recall
  • Professional liability and crime
  • Property: shared and layered
  • Risk management
  • Security risk management
  • Umbrella and excess liability

Noted for high quality service in underwriting, claims and risk engineering, C and F maintains a simple, flexible and cost-effective business infrastructure to meet the needs of our insureds, producers, and shareholders.

Fairmont Specialty

Fairmont Specialty’s property and casualty division offers specialty products and programs designed for select industry segments. Fairmont Specialty’s accident and health division provides medical insurance products either directly or through select program administrators.

Property and casualty segments include:

  • Blasting hazards
  • Oil and gas operators and contractors
  • Propane and petroleum
  • Natural gas and water utilities
  • Retail service stations
  • Septic pumpers
  • Street sweepers
  • Transportation
  • Welding supply dealers

Accident and health products include:

  • Employer stop loss
  • HMO and medical excess reinsurance
  • Organ transplant
  • Retail and group travel
  • Student accident and sickness, and intercollegiate sports
  • Pet accident and sickness
  • Special events
